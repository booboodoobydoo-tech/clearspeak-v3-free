================================================================================
CLEARSPEAK V3 - PRIVACY POLICY
================================================================================

Effective Date: January 2025
Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ClearSpeak V3 ("the Software", "we", "our")
handles your information when you use our application.

================================================================================
1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
================================================================================

ClearSpeak V3 is a desktop application that runs entirely on your computer.
We take your privacy seriously and have designed the Software to minimize data
collection.

INFORMATION STORED LOCALLY ON YOUR COMPUTER:
- Application settings and preferences
- Project files and audio recordings you create
- API credentials (Google Cloud TTS, Gemini AI) that you provide
- Application usage logs (stored locally, not transmitted)

INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT:
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT track your usage remotely
- We do NOT sell or share any data
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services

================================================================================
2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
================================================================================

All data created by or stored in ClearSpeak V3 remains on your local computer.

YOUR DATA IS USED FOR:
- Saving your application preferences
- Storing your projects and recordings
- Connecting to third-party services YOU configure (Google Cloud, Gemini AI)

YOUR DATA IS NEVER:
- Transmitted to our servers (we don't have any servers)
- Shared with third parties (except services you explicitly configure)
- Used for advertising or marketing
- Sold or monetized in any way

================================================================================
3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
================================================================================

ClearSpeak V3 integrates with the following OPTIONAL third-party services.
These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices.

GOOGLE CLOUD TEXT-TO-SPEECH (Optional):
- If you provide Google Cloud credentials, the Software sends text to Google's
  servers to generate speech audio
- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You control what data is sent by choosing what text to convert

GOOGLE GEMINI AI (Optional):
- If you provide a Gemini API key, the Software sends text to Google's AI
  for text correction/improvement
- Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You control what data is sent by choosing when to use AI correction

ELEVENLABS (Optional):
- If you provide ElevenLabs credentials, the Software sends text to ElevenLabs
  for voice generation
- ElevenLabs Privacy Policy: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy
- You control what data is sent by choosing what text to convert

IMPORTANT: These third-party services are OPTIONAL. ClearSpeak V3 will work
without them. If you choose to use them, their respective privacy policies
apply to data you send to their services.

================================================================================
4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY
================================================================================

WHERE YOUR DATA IS STORED:
All ClearSpeak V3 data is stored locally on your computer at:

- macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/clearspeak-v3/
- Windows: %APPDATA%/clearspeak-v3/
- Linux: ~/.config/clearspeak-v3/

HOW YOUR DATA IS PROTECTED:
- Sensitive credentials are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
- Your data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly use third-party
  services (Google Cloud, Gemini AI, ElevenLabs)
- You can delete all application data by removing the directories above

================================================================================
5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES
================================================================================

YOU HAVE THE RIGHT TO:
- View all data stored by ClearSpeak V3 (stored in the directories above)
- Delete all application data at any time
- Choose whether to use third-party services
- Export your projects and recordings to any location
- Use the Software without providing any personal information

HOW TO DELETE YOUR DATA:
1. Uninstall ClearSpeak V3
2. Manually delete the application data folders listed in Section 4
3. Delete any project files you created

================================================================================
6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
================================================================================

ClearSpeak V3 does not collect personal information from anyone, including
children under 13. The Software can be used by children with parental
supervision, but no data is collected or transmitted by us.
